Pulpo a la Gallega

Pulpo a la Gallega is a traditional Galician dish made with octopus, potatoes and paprika. It is often served as a tapa or a main course.

Ingredients

  • 1 large octopus
  • 3 large potatoes
  • 1 large onion
  • olive oil
  • paprika
  • salt

Instructions

  1. Boil the octopus in salted water until tender.
  2. Peel and slice the potatoes into thin pieces.
  3. Chop the onion into thin slices.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook the potatoes until they are tender.
  5. Add the onion and cook for a few more minutes.
  6. Season the octopus with paprika and salt.
  7. Serve the octopus over the potatoes and onions.
