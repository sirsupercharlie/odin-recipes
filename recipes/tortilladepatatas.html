Tortilla de Patatas

Tortilla de patatas, also known as Spanish omelette, is a traditional Spanish dish made with eggs, potatoes and onions. It is often served as a tapa or a main course.

Ingredients

  • 3 large potatoes
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 large onion
  • olive oil
  • salt

Instructions

  1. Peel and slice the potatoes into thin pieces.
  2. Chop the onion into thin slices.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook the potatoes until they are tender.
  4. Add the onion and cook for a few more minutes.
  5. Beat the eggs and season with salt.
  6. Pour the egg mixture over the potatoes and onions.
  7. Cook on low heat until the edges are set.
  8. Flip the tortilla and cook for a few more minutes.
